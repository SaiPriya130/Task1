BAKE A CAKE

Following Steps To Bake A Chocolate Cake

  1. Ingrediants
    1. ¾ cup (170 g) of unsalted butter, softened
    2. ¾ cup (94 g) of unsweetened cocoa powder
    3. ¾ cup (90 g) of flour
    4. ¼ tsp (1 g) of salt
    5. ½ tsp (3 g) of baking powder
    6. 1 cup (225 g) of granulated sugar
    7. 3 eggs, room temperature
    8. 1 teaspoon (4.9 mL) of vanilla extract
    9. 1⁄2 cup (120 mL) of buttermilk or sour cream
  2. Process
    1. Gather your ingredients.
    2. Preheat the oven to 340 °F (171 °C) and grease your cake pan.
    3. Mix the wet ingredients in a large bowl.
    4. Mix the dry ingredients in a separate bowl.
    5. Add the dry mixture to the wet mixture in increments
    6. Pour the batter into the cake pan.
    7. Bake the cake for 30 minutes.
    8. Take the cake out of the oven and let it cool.
    9. Take the cake out of the pan and put it on a plate if you’d like.
    10. Let the cake cool completely before frosting it.
